Dolce Pizza was founded in 1967 by Nick Emanuelo and his son, Nick Emanuelo Jr. Together they began a pizza empire that has survived the ages through dedication to their pizza craft.

Nick Emanuelo Sr. prefers traditional Italian pizza, with fresh basil, olive oil, and mozzerella. Don't forget that he's Italian!

Nick Emanuelo Jr. loves to experiment with his pizza, comibining the best of both American and Italian culture, testing the boundarie- between culture, cuisine, and art
